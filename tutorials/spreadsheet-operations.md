# Spreadsheet Operations

Create and manipulate Excel and CSV files.

## Capabilities

- **Create** Excel (.xlsx) and CSV files
- **Read** and analyze spreadsheet data
- **Modify** cells, rows, columns
- **Calculate** formulas and statistics
- **Format** with styles and colors

## Tips

- Excel files support multiple sheets
- Formulas are preserved when reading/writing
- Large files are processed in chunks
- CSV encoding defaults to UTF-8
