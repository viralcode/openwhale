# visionOS Design Patterns

Spatial computing for Apple Vision Pro.

## Core Principles
- **Spatial:** Content exists in 3D space
- **Immersive:** Blend digital and physical
- **Natural:** Intuitive eye + hand interaction

## Windows & Spaces
- **Windows:** Float in space, resizable
- **Volumes:** 3D content (models, games)
- **Full Spaces:** Fully immersive environments

## Interaction
- **Gaze:** Look at elements to target
- **Pinch:** Primary selection gesture (thumb + index finger)
- **Hand gestures:** Natural, tracked in space
- **Voice:** Siri commands

## Materials
- **Glass:** Background material with vibrancy
- **Depth:** Layers in 3D space
- **Shadows:** Grounded in environment

## Typography
- **SF Pro:** System font
- **Legibility:** Designed for spatial context
- **Sizing:** Based on distance from user

## Immersion
- **Dial:** Control environment blend
- **Passthrough:** See real world
- **Full Immersion:** Complete digital environment

## Spatial Audio
- **3D positional audio:** Sounds placed in space
- **Environmental:** Responds to head movement
- **Immersive:** Surrounds user
