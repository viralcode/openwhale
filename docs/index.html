SYSTEM OVERVIEW

OpenWhale is a self-extensible local AI assistant designed for developers and power users who want their AI to take action, not just chat. It acts as an autonomous layer between your LLMs and your operating system.

Unlike standard chatbots, OpenWhale can control your browser, execute code, manage files, and interact with your messaging apps (WhatsApp, Telegram, etc.) bi-directionally. It supports hot-swapping between 8+ AI providers including Anthropic, OpenAI, and local Llamas.
